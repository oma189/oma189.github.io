Blockchain Game

With the play-to-earn gaming era swiftly approaching, the quest to build the best game with a digital asset ecosystem is on. As of now, we’ve seen very few games released that have modern game quality as well as the satisfaction that we get from traditional games, but many are looking to make this a reality.

Ownership

What’s important to note is that NFTs by themselves are not asset classes, but rather what they’re attached to. An NFT by itself is a technological method for proving ownership in a tamper-proof way. It’s a record of ownership that cannot be manipulated as its existence is stamped on the blockchain - a public ledger for recording data of all sorts.
